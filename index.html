📖 How Drivers Use This
1. Dispatcher uploads Yard List (Tab 1) and LoadSumm (Tab 2).
2. Share this page URL with drivers via WhatsApp/text.
3. Driver opens on phone browser → clicks Driver View tab.
4. Driver types their Booking # → sees their next container, exact location (inc. Door #), seal # and weight.
5. Driver clicks ✓ Confirm Pickup, enters their Driver #. Container moves to dispatched. Next driver sees the next in line.
6. If container not accessible, driver clicks ⚠ Can't Access — picks a reason. Container is placed on Hold and visible to dispatcher in Tab 2 for resolution.
